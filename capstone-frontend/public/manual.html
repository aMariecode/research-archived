Browsing Projects

  • All approved capstone projects are displayed on the Home page
  • Use the year tabs to filter projects by batch year
  • Use the sort dropdown to arrange by newest, oldest, or title
  • Click "View Details" on any project card to see more information

Searching

  • Type keywords in the search bar at the top of the home page
  • Search by project title, author name, or topic
  • Press Enter or click the Search button

Viewing & Downloading

1

Click "View Details" on a project card

2

Click "View PDF" to read the document in your browser

3

Click "Download" to save the PDF to your device

Creating an Account

1

Click "Sign In" in the top-right corner

2

Select the "Register" tab

3

Enter your name, email, and password, then click "Create Account"

You can also sign in quickly with your Google account.

Signing In

  • Go to the Sign In page
  • Enter your email and password
  • Click "Sign In"

For Administrators

  • Sign in with your admin account
  • Click your name → "Admin Dashboard"
  • From the dashboard, you can add, edit, or delete capstone projects
  • View analytics and manage users

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues, contact the University of the Assumption IT Department or your faculty adviser.
