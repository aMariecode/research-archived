About UA Research Archive

The University of the Assumption Research Archive is a comprehensive digital repository dedicated to preserving and showcasing the innovative projects developed by students in the Bachelor of Information Technology program.

This archive serves as a testament to the academic excellence and creativity of our IT students, providing easy access to research papers, technical documentation, and project portfolios.
