# ADMIN MANUAL
## Research Archived: Capstone Repository System

---

## How to Login as Admin

Step 1: Go to the website.

Step 2: Click "Login".

Step 3: Enter your Admin Email and Password.

Step 4: Click "Login" button.

---

## How to View Dashboard

Step 1: After login, you will see the Admin Dashboard.

Step 2: View statistics: Total Capstones, Archived, Total Users, Downloads.

---

## How to View All Capstones

Step 1: Click "Capstones" in the sidebar menu.

Step 2: Browse the list of all capstones.

---

## How to Add a New Capstone

Step 1: Click "Add Capstone" button.

Step 2: Fill in the details:
- Title
- Abstract
- Members
- Adviser
- Year
- Technologies
- PDF File
- GitHub URL (optional)

Step 3: Click "Submit" button.

---

## How to Edit a Capstone

Step 1: Find the capstone in the list.

Step 2: Click "Edit" button.

Step 3: Update the information.

Step 4: Click "Save" button.

---

## How to Delete a Capstone

Step 1: Find the capstone in the list.

Step 2: Click "Delete" button.

Step 3: Confirm the deletion.

---

## How to View All Users

Step 1: Click "Users" in the sidebar menu.

Step 2: View the list of registered users (Name, Email, Role, Status).

---

## How to Change Password

Step 1: Click "Settings" in the sidebar menu.

Step 2: Enter your Current Password.

Step 3: Enter your New Password.

Step 4: Enter Confirm New Password.

Step 5: Click "Update Password" button.

---

## How to View Library

Step 1: Click "View Library" in the sidebar menu.

Step 2: You will be redirected to the public library page.

Step 3: Browse capstones as a regular user would see them.

---

## How to Logout

Step 1: Click the dropdown arrow next to your name.

Step 2: Click "Logout".

Step 3: You are now logged out.
