## 1. System Overview

**Research Archived** is a web-based capstone repository system developed for the University of the Assumption. It serves as a digital archive for capstone projects from the Bachelor of Information Technology program.

**Purpose:**
- Store and organize approved capstone projects
- Allow students and faculty to browse and search research works
- Provide easy access to capstone documents in PDF format

**System Features:**
- Browse capstone projects by year
- Search projects by title, author, or keyword
- View project details (title, abstract, members, adviser)
- View and download PDF documents

**System Requirements:**
- Web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
- Internet connection

**Website URL:** `https://research-archived.vercel.app`
