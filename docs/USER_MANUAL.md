## 1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Introduction

**Research Archived** is a web-based capstone repository system designed to store, organize, and provide access to capstone projects within the university. The system serves as a centralized digital library where students, faculty, and researchers can easily browse, search, and access past capstone works for reference and research purposes.

### 1.2 Purpose

The system aims to:
- Provide a centralized repository for archiving capstone projects
- Enable easy search and retrieval of past research works
- Promote knowledge sharing among students and faculty
- Preserve academic works in digital format for future reference

### 1.3 Key Features for Users

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Browse Capstones** | View the complete list of approved capstone projects |
| **Search** | Find capstones using keywords (title, abstract, author, technologies) |
| **Filter** | Narrow down results by year or adviser |
| **View Details** | Access complete information about each capstone project |
| **View PDF** | Read the full capstone document online |
| **Download** | Save PDF copies of capstone documents |
| **Profile Management** | Update personal information and change password |

### 1.4 System Requirements

To use Research Archived, you need:
- A device with internet connection (computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone)
- A modern web browser (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, or Safari)
- A valid email address for registration

### 1.5 User Roles

| Role | Access Level |
|------|--------------|
| **Guest** | Can browse and view approved capstones without logging in |
| **Registered User** | Can browse, search, filter, view, download, and manage profile |

## 2. GETTING STARTED

### 2.1 Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser
2. Enter the website URL in the address bar
3. Press Enter to load the system

### 2.2 Creating an Account

1. Click the **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"** button
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name** – Enter your complete name
   - **Email** – Enter a valid email address
   - **Password** – Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
3. Click **"Register"** to create your account
4. You will be automatically logged in upon successful registration

### 2.3 Logging In

1. Click the **"Login"** button
2. Enter your registered **Email** address
3. Enter your **Password**
4. Click **"Login"** to access the system

### 2.4 Google Sign-In

1. Click the **"Sign in with Google"** button
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant permission if prompted
4. You will be automatically logged into the system

## 4. BROWSING CAPSTONES

### 4.1 Viewing Capstone List

1. Navigate to the **Capstones** section
2. Browse through the list of approved projects
3. Each entry displays:
   - Title
   - Authors/Members
   - Year
   - Adviser

### 4.2 Viewing Capstone Details

1. Click on a capstone title to view full details
2. The details page shows:
   - **Title** – The capstone project title
   - **Abstract** – Summary of the research
   - **Members** – Names of the researchers
   - **Adviser** – Faculty adviser name
   - **Year** – Year of completion
   - **Technologies** – Tools and technologies used
   - **GitHub URL** – Link to source code (if available)
   - **PDF Document** – The full capstone document

---

## 5. SEARCHING AND FILTERING

### 5.1 Using the Search Function

1. Locate the search bar
2. Enter keywords related to:
   - Capstone title
   - Abstract content
   - Author/member names
   - Adviser name
   - Technologies used
3. Press Enter or click the search icon
4. View the matching results

### 5.2 Filtering by Year

1. Locate the **Year** filter dropdown
2. Select the desired year
3. The list will update to show only capstones from that year

### 5.3 Filtering by Adviser

1. Locate the **Adviser** filter
2. Select or enter the adviser's name
3. View capstones supervised by that adviser

---

## 6. VIEWING AND DOWNLOADING PDF

### 6.1 Viewing PDF Documents

1. Open the capstone details page
2. Click the **"View PDF"** button
3. The PDF will open in a new tab or PDF viewer

### 6.2 Downloading PDF Files

1. Open the capstone details page
2. Click the **"Download"** button
3. The PDF file will be saved to your device

---

## 7. MANAGING YOUR PROFILE

### 7.1 Viewing Your Profile

1. Click on your name or **Profile** in the navigation menu
2. View your account information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Account Role

### 7.2 Updating Your Information

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click **"Edit Profile"**
3. Update your **Full Name** or **Email**
4. Click **"Save"** to apply changes

### 7.3 Changing Your Password

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your **Current Password**
4. Enter your **New Password**
5. Confirm your **New Password**
6. Click **"Update Password"**

---

## 8. LOGGING OUT

1. Click **"Logout"** in the navigation menu
2. You will be signed out and redirected to the home page

## 9. TROUBLESHOOTING

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Cannot login | Check if email and password are correct. Ensure Caps Lock is off. |
| Forgot password | Contact the administrator to reset your password |
| PDF not loading | Try refreshing the page or use a different browser |
| Search returns no results | Try different keywords or check spelling |
| Page not loading | Check your internet connection and refresh the page |
| Google login not working | Ensure pop-ups are enabled and try again |
